# Privacy Policy — Linux Lab

_Last updated: 2026_

Linux Lab ("the app") is operated by Qup DA. This policy explains what we
collect and why.

## What we collect

- **Account data:** your email address and a securely hashed password.
- **Saved SSH hosts (optional):** hostnames, ports, usernames, and credentials
  you choose to save. Credentials are **encrypted at rest** (AES-256-GCM) and
  are never shown back to you or shared.
- **Session metadata:** start/end times, session type, and resource counters.
  We do **not** record the contents of your terminal sessions (no keystrokes,
  no output).
- **Technical data:** IP address and timestamps, used for rate limiting and
  abuse prevention.

## What we do NOT collect

- We do not log or store the contents of your terminal/SSH sessions.
- We do not sell your data or use it for advertising.

## How we use it

- To provide the service (authentication, running sessions, SSH connections).
- To prevent abuse (rate limiting, banning).

## Retention & deletion

- You can delete your account at any time from the app (Settings → Delete
  account). This removes your user record, saved hosts, and session metadata.

## Security

- Passwords are hashed (bcrypt). Saved SSH credentials are encrypted at rest.
- Traffic is encrypted in transit (TLS/HTTPS/WSS).
